ABOUT

Service Economy Applications, Inc. (SEAI) was incorporated by the Securities and Exchange Commission of the Philippines on March 27, 2018, under Company Registration No. CS 201804315. SEAI is a vibrant organization composed of developers and corporate professionals from diverse backgrounds, all committed to delivering innovative solutions to meet critical needs in the Philippines.

SEAI is dedicated to continuous growth and maintaining its leadership in the industry. Recently, the company expanded through a strategic partnership with DAHUA, enabling SEAI to provide state-of-the-art SMART CAMPUS IT Solutions to universities and colleges throughout the Philippines. These advanced solutions help institutions improve security, streamline operations, and create a more connected learning environment.

Extending its dedication to innovation, SEAI is now focusing on jail management. By developing advanced technological solutions, SEAI aims to transform the management and operations of correctional facilities in the Philippines. These innovations will streamline processes, improve security, and support rehabilitation
